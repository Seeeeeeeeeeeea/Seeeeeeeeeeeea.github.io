2025年5月 May 2025

进入课题组，开始科研实习

Joined Research Group, Started Research Internship

2025年5月，我加入西安电子科技大学计算机科学与技术学院苗启广教授团队，开始进行科研实习。我的科研起点是参与“面部属性可编辑的说话头视频生成算法”相关研究。

在该项目后期，我主要负责论文展示图的制作以及项目主页展示视频的制作。同时，我在本地复现了相关方法，完成模型推理，并参与后续论文的比较分析工作。随着论文经历一系列转投和修改过程，我逐渐参与到更深入的论文修改与撰写工作中，并根据审稿人意见完成了多轮论文内容调整。

这一阶段对我而言是从“参与科研”到“理解科研流程”的起点。通过该项目，我学会了使用 Markdown 整理科研笔记，掌握了基于 LaTeX 的科技论文写作流程，并熟悉了使用 Zotero 进行文献管理和论文引用整理的方法。

In May 2025, I joined Professor Qiguang Miao's team at the School of Computer Science and Technology, XIDIAN University, and began my research internship. My research started with participating in the study of "facial attribute-editable talking head video generation algorithms".

During the late stage of this project, I was primarily responsible for creating figures for the paper and the demo video for the project homepage. Meanwhile, I reproduced the relevant methods locally, completed model inference, and participated in the comparative analysis for the subsequent paper. As the paper went through a series of resubmissions and revisions, I gradually got involved in deeper paper revision and writing, and completed multiple rounds of content adjustments based on reviewers' comments.

For me, this stage was the starting point from "participating in research" to "understanding the research workflow". Through this project, I learned to use Markdown to organize research notes, mastered the LaTeX-based scientific paper writing process, and became familiar with using Zotero for literature management and citation organization.

Supplemented Deep Learning Basics, Deeply Understood Diffusion Models and Talking Head Methods

2025年暑假期间，我集中学习了深度学习基础知识，观看了李沐和李宏毅的深度学习课程，系统补充了神经网络、深度学习训练机制以及生成模型相关基础。

在此期间，我也开始阅读领域内经典论文，例如 AniPortrait、Hallo 等开源说话头生成方法，并对前期参与的面部属性可编辑说话头视频生成算法进行了更深入的代码阅读。通过这一过程，我逐渐熟悉了 UNet 架构、扩散模型的基本原理，以及条件控制在生成任务中的作用方式。

这一阶段的核心意义在于，我不再只是执行具体实验或制作展示材料，而是开始理解模型结构、训练逻辑和方法设计背后的原因，为后续独立思考研究问题打下了基础。

During the summer vacation of 2025, I intensively studied the basics of deep learning. I watched the deep learning courses by Mu Li and Hung-yi Lee, systematically supplementing my knowledge of neural networks, deep learning training mechanisms, and generative models.

During this period, I also began reading classic papers in the field, such as open-source talking head generation methods like AniPortrait and Hallo, and deeply read the code of the facial attribute-editable talking head video generation algorithm I previously participated in. Through this process, I gradually became familiar with the UNet architecture, the basic principles of diffusion models, and how conditional control works in generative tasks.

The core significance of this stage is that I am no longer just executing specific experiments or making presentation materials, but began to understand the model structure, training logic, and the reasons behind method design, laying a foundation for independently thinking about research problems later.

2025年9月 Sept 2025

从纯关键点半身数字人生成出发，发现身份泄露问题

Started from Pure Keypoint-based Half-body Generation, Discovered the Identity Leakage Problem

在进一步的科研实践中，我们最初希望研究基于纯关键点的跨身份半身数字人生成方法，对标 AnimateAnyone 和 MimicMotion 等方法，并以 AniPortrait 后半部分的 pose2video 模块作为基座方法进行实现。

然而，后续训练结果显示，当使用全量关键点进行跨身份驱动时，模型容易出现身份泄露和身份偏移问题。也就是说，驱动关键点不仅携带动作信息，还可能隐含原始驱动视频中的身份特征，从而干扰参考图所提供的身份信息。

基于这一现象，我们逐渐明确了一个核心问题：如果要实现具有泛化能力的半身数字人视频生成，必须解决“身份信息应该从哪里来、动作信息应该从哪里来”的责任划分问题。具体来说，可以有两条路线：

第一条路线是让生成视频与参考图保持身份对齐，即将驱动关键点中的身份信息主动对齐到参考图身份上；第二条路线是让驱动关键点本身尽可能不包含身份信息，从源头上减少关键点对身份的干扰。

In further research practice, we initially hoped to study cross-identity half-body digital human generation methods based purely on keypoints, benchmarking against methods like AnimateAnyone and MimicMotion, and implemented it using the pose2video module in the second half of AniPortrait as the base method.

However, subsequent training results showed that when using full keypoints for cross-identity driving, the model is prone to identity leakage and identity shift problems. That is to say, the driving keypoints not only carry motion information but also may imply the identity features from the original driving video, thereby interfering with the identity information provided by the reference image.

Based on this phenomenon, we gradually clarified a core problem: if we want to achieve half-body digital human video generation with generalization capabilities, we must solve the responsibility division problem of "where the identity information should come from and where the motion information should come from". Specifically, there can be two routes:

The first route is to keep the generated video aligned with the reference image's identity, that is, actively align the identity information in the driving keypoints to the reference image's identity; the second route is to make the driving keypoints themselves contain as little identity information as possible, reducing the interference of keypoints on identity from the source.

2025年10月 - 12月 Oct - Dec 2025

尝试通过 3D 表示对齐身份信息

Attempted to Align Identity Information via 3D Representations

第一条路线的核心思路是将驱动关键点的身份与参考图身份进行对齐。我们当时考虑的是“音频到 3D 模型，再到 2D 关键点，最后到视频生成”的技术路线。

受到 AniPortrait 的启发，我们进一步考虑通过 Audio2Mesh 的方式，将参考图身份融合到 3D 表示中，再转换为 2D 关键点。随后，我们找到了经典方法 EMAGE。该方法可以实现音频驱动关键点生成，并且关键点中的面部轮廓信息，也就是身份相关信息，可以由一组参数 betas 控制。原则上，如果能够从参考图中提取这组 betas，并将其替换到音频驱动生成的 pose 中，就有可能实现身份统一。

围绕这一思路，我开展了具体实现工作：在本地部署 OpenPose 以提取对应关键点，学习了经典 3D 人体建模方法 SMPL-X，并复现了人体体态估计相关方法，包括 SMPLify-X、SMPLer-X 和 BLADE 等。

但在实际操作过程中，我们发现该路线存在较高成本。我们的目标任务是半身场景下的数字人生成，而这些方法通常基于全身数据进行训练，生成的关键点也更适合全身人体场景。在半身数字人任务中直接使用时并不完全适配，可能需要修改网络架构，或者增加针对半身场景的标注数据重新训练，整体成本较大。因此，这一方向在实验验证后被暂时放弃。

The core idea of the first route is to align the identity of the driving keypoints with the reference image's identity. At that time, we considered the technical route of "audio to 3D model, then to 2D keypoints, and finally to video generation".

Inspired by AniPortrait, we further considered integrating the reference image's identity into a 3D representation via an Audio2Mesh approach, and then converting it to 2D keypoints. Subsequently, we found the classic method EMAGE. This method can generate audio-driven keypoints, and the facial contour information in the keypoints, which is identity-related information, can be controlled by a set of parameters called betas. In principle, if we could extract this set of betas from the reference image and replace it into the pose generated by audio drive, it might be possible to achieve identity unification.

Around this idea, I carried out specific implementation work: deployed OpenPose locally to extract corresponding keypoints, learned the classic 3D human modeling method SMPL-X, and reproduced human body estimation-related methods, including SMPLify-X, SMPLer-X, and BLADE.

But in actual operation, we found that this route has a relatively high cost. Our target task is digital human generation in half-body scenarios, while these methods are typically trained based on full-body data, and the generated keypoints are also more suitable for full-body human scenarios. They are not perfectly adaptable when used directly in half-body digital human tasks, potentially requiring modifying the network architecture or adding annotation data for half-body scenarios to retrain, which overall costs a lot. Therefore, this direction was temporarily abandoned after experimental verification.

2026年1月 - 5月 Jan - May 2026

探索不携带身份信息的关键点，实现解耦式半身数字人生成

Explored Identity-Free Keypoints, Achieved Decoupled Half-body Generation

随后，我们转向第二条路线：使用尽可能不携带身份信息的关键点进行驱动。

根据前期实验，我们已经知道面部稠密关键点可能导致身份泄露。因此，最直接的思路就是逐步去掉面部稠密点，并观察不同关键点设置对生成结果的影响。我们依次进行了多组测试，包括去掉面部轮廓、去掉面部所有稠密点、去掉头部骨骼点等。

最终，受到 EchoMimicV2 和 V-Express 的启发，我们认为一种更合理的条件划分方式是：使用头部三个关键点控制头部朝向，使用手部关键点控制手部动作，使用音频控制面部动态，尤其是嘴部运动，而参考图则只负责提供身份信息。

这种设计的关键在于让不同条件各司其职：关键点不再包含明显身份信息，参考图可以自由切换，模型也不容易出现身份过度偏移，从而实现跨身份泛化生成。

与此同时，我们也观察到关键点作为强条件时，可能会压制音频对面部动态，尤其是嘴部区域的控制。针对这一问题，我们引入了条件丢弃操作：随机将带有头部三点的关键点替换为不带头部三点的关键点，使模型在训练过程中学习如何平衡头部结构控制与音频动态控制之间的关系。

为了支撑模型训练，我们进行了大量数据采集，覆盖头部视频和半身视频，并使用关键点检测方法以及 DOVER 美学检测方法对视频进行筛选。在此基础上，我们进一步开展了音频编码器相关实验，分别测试了 Whisper + linear projection、Wav2Vec2 + Q-Former、Whisper 裸接等多种方式，以探索最适合当前任务的音频注入方式。最终结果显示，Whisper 裸接方法对嘴部驱动效果更加有效。

综上，我们主要解决了两个问题。

第一个问题是，以 EchoMimicV2 为代表的方法在实现跨身份半身数字人泛化生成时，缺乏头部结构化控制条件，导致音频驱动的头部动作容易出现僵硬和抖动。我们通过引入头部三点关键点作为轻量结构控制信号，增强了头部姿态控制能力。

第二个问题是，半身数字人在多语言音频驱动场景下仍然存在不足。我们通过大量收集多语言数据，并结合不同音频编码方式实验，提升了模型在多语言语音驱动下的嘴部同步能力。

基于上述方法，我们可以实现跨身份数字人生成，也就是类似 AI 换脸的参考身份替换；也可以实现嘴部编辑，即在相同动作下更换说话语音；还可以实现动作编辑，即在同一参考身份下更换驱动关键点，从而生成不同动作表现的视频。

Subsequently, we turned to the second route: driving with keypoints that carry as little identity information as possible.

According to preliminary experiments, we already knew that dense facial keypoints might lead to identity leakage. Therefore, the most direct idea is to gradually remove the dense facial points and observe the impact of different keypoint settings on the generation results. We sequentially conducted multiple sets of tests, including removing facial contours, removing all dense facial points, removing head skeleton points, etc.

Ultimately, inspired by EchoMimicV2 and V-Express, we believe a more reasonable conditional division is: using three head keypoints to control head orientation, hand keypoints to control hand motion, audio to control facial dynamics, especially mouth movements, while the reference image is only responsible for providing identity information.

The key to this design is to let different conditions perform their own duties: keypoints no longer contain obvious identity information, the reference image can be freely switched, and the model is less prone to excessive identity shift, thereby achieving cross-identity generalized generation.

At the same time, we also observed that when keypoints act as strong conditions, they might suppress the audio's control over facial dynamics, especially the mouth area. To address this problem, we introduced a condition dropout operation: randomly replacing keypoints with the three head points with keypoints without them, allowing the model to learn how to balance the relationship between head structure control and audio dynamic control during the training process.

To support model training, we conducted a massive amount of data collection, covering head videos and half-body videos, and used keypoint detection methods and the DOVER aesthetic detection method to filter the videos. On this basis, we further carried out audio encoder-related experiments, respectively testing various methods such as Whisper + linear projection, Wav2Vec2 + Q-Former, and bare Whisper, to explore the most suitable audio injection method for the current task. The final results showed that the bare Whisper method is more effective for mouth driving.

In summary, we mainly solved two problems.

The first problem is that methods represented by EchoMimicV2 lack structured head control conditions when achieving cross-identity half-body digital human generalized generation, leading to stiff and jittery head movements driven by audio. By introducing three head keypoints as a lightweight structural control signal, we enhanced the head pose control capability.

The second problem is that half-body digital humans still have deficiencies in multilingual audio-driven scenarios. By collecting a large amount of multilingual data and experimenting with different audio encoding methods, we improved the model's mouth synchronization capability under multilingual speech driving.

Based on the above methods, we can achieve cross-identity digital human generation, which is similar to reference identity replacement in AI face swapping; we can also achieve mouth editing, replacing speaking voice under the same motion; and we can also achieve motion editing, replacing driving keypoints under the same reference identity to generate videos with different motion performances.

个人思考

Personal Reflections

在前期科研推进过程中，我遇到了不少问题。最明显的感受是，在思考创新点时，我常常会感到迷茫，不知道应该从哪里入手。后来我逐渐意识到，创新点并不是凭空想出来的，而是需要通过大量阅读论文、理解领域问题，并结合实验现象不断验证和沉淀出来的。从论文中，可以获得其他方法对于问题的理解方式，也可以将相关领域的 insight 迁移到当前任务中；从实验中，则可以发现一些原本没有被清晰表达出来的现象。这些现象如果能够被解释、被验证，并进一步转化为方法设计，就可能形成真正有价值的创新点。

在这一过程中，我通过实践逐渐掌握了扩散模型的基本原理，也通过当前接触到的方法获得了很多启发。比如，相比于扩散模型中的 CFG 策略（通常在推理阶段放大某个条件），条件 Dropout 的思想更关注训练过程中的条件分配问题——它不是简单地在推理时加强某个条件，而是在训练时让模型学会不同条件之间应该如何分工。结合我的实践来看，条件 Dropout 的核心作用就是权衡用于控制头部朝向的关键点和用于面部动态的音频之间的关系，从而实现条件解耦。这个方法非常符合我对好方法“大道至简”的理解，它对后续多条件解耦控制任务也具有启发意义，尤其适用于多个条件间存在冲突或控制权竞争的场景。

另外，我对扩散时间步这一方向的 insight 非常感兴趣。在阅读 T2I-Adapter 等文献时我发现，扩散模型在不同去噪时间步中具有不同的作用粒度：扩散早期更决定结构和运动，后期更偏向细节修复。扩散模型就像一个画家，早期先勾勒出整体轮廓，后期再不断补充纹理与细节。围绕这一现象我也进行了实验验证：如果在扩散早期引入头部三点结构信息来引导头部朝向，生成结果几乎和全程引导没有明显差异；而如果只在中期或后期引入则无法达到同样效果。这说明不同条件可能应该在不同时间步承担不同责任，结构类条件更适合在早期发挥作用，细节类条件更适合在后期发挥作用。如何根据条件类型设计时间步注入策略，是一个值得继续探索的问题。

此外，我也重新思考了“音频到动作、动作到视频”的技术路线。这个思路很快被同期工作（如 CyberHost、VersaAnimator）验证。我认为，相比于让音频直接作用在 latent 上，先由音频生成动作，再由动作驱动视频生成，可能更加合理且贴近真实应用场景。例如在新闻口播场景中，用户不可能手动生成关键点，更合理的方案是由音频自动生成对应的关键点动作。这种音频驱动动作的 pose generation 任务，未来也极有可能与具身智能（如机器人随节拍做动作）产生交叉。

最后，我也关注扩散时间步压缩问题。当前业界已经开始使用 DiT 架构进行扩散时间步压缩以实现实时生成，但其缺点是算力需求较大。相比之下，我认为对更加轻量化的 UNet 架构进行扩散时间步压缩探索，同样具有巨大的应用价值。它可能不一定追求极高的性能上限，但更适合在计算资源受限的真实场景中部署和使用。

During the early stages of research, I encountered many problems. My most obvious feeling was that when thinking about innovative points, I often felt confused. Later, I gradually realized that innovative points are not thought up out of thin air, but need to be constantly verified and accumulated through extensive reading of papers, understanding domain problems, and combining with experimental phenomena. From papers, you can get how other methods understand the problem and transfer insights to the current task; from experiments, you can discover phenomena that were not clearly expressed originally. If these phenomena can be explained and verified, they may form truly valuable innovative points.

During this process, through practice, I gradually mastered the basic principles of diffusion models and gained a lot of inspiration. For example, compared to the CFG strategy (which usually amplifies a condition during inference), the idea of condition Dropout pays more attention to condition allocation. It doesn't simply strengthen a certain condition during inference, but lets the model learn how different conditions should divide the work during training. Combined with my practice, the core role of condition Dropout is to balance the relationship between keypoints for head orientation and audio for facial dynamics, thereby achieving condition decoupling. This method aligns with my understanding of a good method being "profoundly simple" and has heuristic significance for multi-condition control tasks with conflicts.

In addition, I am very interested in the insight regarding diffusion timesteps. When reading related literature (like T2I-Adapter), I found that diffusion models have different action granularities in different denoising timesteps: the early diffusion stage mostly determines structure and motion, while the later stage leans towards detail restoration. A diffusion model is like a painter: first sketching the overall outline in the early stages, and adding details in the later stages. I also conducted experimental verification: if structural information is introduced in the early stage to guide head orientation, the result is almost identical to full-process guidance; but if only introduced in the late stages, it fails. This indicates that different conditions should take different responsibilities at different timesteps; structural conditions are more suitable for early stages, while detail conditions are for later stages. How to design a timestep injection strategy based on condition types is a highly valuable question.

Furthermore, I rethought the technical route of "audio to motion, motion to video". This idea was soon verified by concurrent works (like CyberHost, VersaAnimator). I think that compared to letting audio directly act on the latent space, first generating motion from audio and then driving video generation from motion is more reasonable and closer to real application scenarios. For example, in a news anchor scenario, a more reasonable way is to automatically generate keypoint motions from audio rather than manually plotting them. Such audio-driven pose generation tasks may also intersect with embodied intelligence (such as a robot making movements to rhythm) in the future.

Finally, I also pay attention to diffusion timestep compression. Currently, the industry uses the DiT architecture for compression to achieve real-time generation, but its computational requirement is quite high. In contrast, I believe exploring diffusion timestep compression on the more lightweight UNet architecture is equally valuable for practical applications. It might not pursue the extreme highest performance, but is much more suitable for deployment in real-world scenarios with restricted computational resources.
